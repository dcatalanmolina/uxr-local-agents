**INS-001 — Executive Summary**

Customers are leaving at the final step of checkout, costing the business revenue, primarily due to confusion around the billing address form.  A significant 64% drop-off rate is observed on mobile compared to desktop, with a notable increase in mobile drop-off from 48% in Q3 2024.  This issue, exacerbated by usability sessions documenting participant frustration with mobile form elements, indicates a specific UX failure at the billing address screen. The mobile drop-off rate is the largest single point of concern, suggesting a responsive form layout is critical. This is not a trust or pricing issue; it’s a fundamental usability problem demanding immediate attention.
