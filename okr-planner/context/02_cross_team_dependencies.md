# Cross-Team Dependencies

## Legal
Legal reviews all external-facing contracts, partner agreements, and marketing
offers that include discounts or financial terms. Current SLA is 3 weeks for
new submissions. Any work requiring Legal sign-off must be flagged at least
4 weeks before the desired launch date. Primary contact: Jamie (Legal Counsel).

## Engineering (Priya's team)
Product growth initiatives that require A/B testing, new onboarding flows, or
instrumentation changes must go through Priya's team. Engineering operates on
2-week sprints. Requests need to be scoped and submitted at least one sprint
in advance. Priya is the decision-maker for engineering prioritization.

## Sales (Marcus's team)
Enterprise deals affect activation numbers and MRR simultaneously. Sarah's
retention campaigns require budget approval from Marcus. Marcus's deals
generate users that flow into Sarah's activation and churn metrics.

## Data / Analytics
The analytics function sits inside Engineering. Data requests (custom reports,
funnel analysis, cohort breakdowns) go to Priya, who assigns them within
the team. Turnaround is typically 1 week for ad hoc queries.

## Marketing
Marketing owns top-of-funnel campaigns. They are not part of the Growth team
but coordinate on ICP definition and messaging. Contact: Dana (Head of Marketing).
Misalignment with Marketing on ICP directly affects NPS and activation rates.
