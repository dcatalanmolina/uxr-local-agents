# Team Role

We are the Growth team at a B2B SaaS company. Our mandate is to drive user
acquisition, activation, and retention across all product lines. We own the
full funnel from awareness through activation and renewal.

The team currently has three functional leads: Sarah (Product Growth), Marcus
(Sales), and Priya (Engineering). Each owns a distinct part of the growth
motion but all three are interdependent — engineering velocity directly affects
product growth, and product growth directly affects revenue.

This quarter is a critical one. The company is 18 months post-launch and under
pressure from the board to demonstrate sustainable growth before a Series B
raise planned for Q3 2025.
